Open Security Exchange

A blockchain-based exchange for virtual securities

Introducing Open Security Exchange

OpenSecurityExchange is a platform for issuing, trading, and managing Bitcoin-nominated virtual securities. It utilizes the blockchain to achieve a higher level of security than exchanges using username-password systems. It also inherits both anonymity and transparency from the blockchain.

Security

OpenSecurityExchange uses the blockchain as its protocol. Your Bitcoin address is your account. One can never access an account until having its private key.

Anonymity

Even we ourselves do not know your information except the Bitcoin address. No e-mails, usernames, or passwords are stored in our database, nor they are used at all.

Transparency

The trading are encoded in the blockchain. All trading history is accessible by anyone. No front-runs or secret trades are possible.
